- ❌ Not supported

## Service ↔ Model Detail

### Speed Limit Removal

All models with firmware-enforced speed limits can be unlocked. This is most commonly requested for:

- **Xiaomi** models (factory limited to 25 km/h, can be raised to 32-35 km/h)
- **Segway-Ninebot** models (factory limited to 20-30 km/h depending on region)
- **Performance scooters** (Dualtron, Kaabo, etc.) may have region-locked firmware that limits speed

### Firmware Update

Custom firmware availability varies by brand:

- **Xiaomi:** Extensive custom firmware community (CFW via ScooterHacking, XiaoFlasher)
- **Segway-Ninebot:** Good custom firmware support (via NinebotFlasher tools)
- **Dualtron/Kaabo/VSETT/Zero:** Usually updated via manufacturer tools; less custom firmware
- **Inokim:** Limited firmware customization options (mostly factory updates)

### Cruise Control

- Most modern scooters support cruise control activation via firmware
- Some require hardware modifications (additional button/switch)
- Xiaomi and Segway-Ninebot: typically firmware-only activation
- Performance scooters: often built-in but may need configuration

### General Maintenance

Universal service available for all brands:

- Tire changes (pneumatic and solid)
- Brake pad replacement and adjustment
- Battery health diagnostics
- Stem/folding mechanism tightening
- Waterproofing checks
- Controller diagnostics
- Light/reflector replacement

## Technician Specialization

Technicians specify which brands they support in their profile. A technician doesn't need to support all brands — they can specialize. The platform filters technician results based on the user's scooter model.
