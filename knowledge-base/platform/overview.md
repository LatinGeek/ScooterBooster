# ScooterBooster — Platform Overview

## Mission
Conectar a los dueños de scooters eléctricos en Uruguay con técnicos especializados para potenciar, actualizar y mantener sus vehículos.

**English:** Connect electric scooter owners in Uruguay with specialized technicians to boost, update, and maintain their vehicles.

## Product
ScooterBooster is a two-sided marketplace:
- **Supply side:** Verified technicians who offer scooter modification and maintenance services
- **Demand side:** Scooter owners looking for performance upgrades, firmware updates, and maintenance

## Domain
- **Website:** scooterbooster.uy
- **Language:** Spanish (all user-facing content)
- **Currency:** Uruguayan Peso (UYU)

## Target Market
- **Country:** Uruguay
- **Primary cities:** Montevideo, Punta del Este, Maldonado, Colonia del Sacramento
- **Target demographic:**
  - Age: 18-45
  - Tech-savvy urban commuters
  - Electric scooter enthusiasts
  - Both casual riders and performance enthusiasts

## Tagline
- **Primary:** "Potenciá tu scooter" (Boost your scooter)
- **Secondary:** "Conectamos dueños de scooters con los mejores técnicos de Uruguay"

## Key Differentiators
1. **Uruguay-first:** Tailored for the Uruguayan market, UYU pricing, local regulations
2. **Verified technicians:** Admin-approved technicians with ratings and reviews
3. **Comprehensive services:** From speed limit mods to basic maintenance
4. **Legal compliance:** Clear disclaimers for speed modifications (private use only)
5. **WhatsApp integration:** Direct communication channel familiar to Uruguayan users
